ArcGIS ModelBuilder

This model compares floodplain with tax parcel data to identify what urban areas are likely to be impacted by a flooding event. (Click the images to view full size.)

By entering two shapefiles, a user may apply this model to any city. Outputs include tables of statistical data and a map. The map shows tax parcels that intersect the regulated floodplain (shown in red) and tax parcels within 500 feet of the floodplain (shown in orange).

The examples above were rendered in ArcMap. The example below is hosted by ArcGIS Online.

Geospatial Data: Pierce County Open GeoSpatial Data Portal, King County GIS Open Data, New York State GIS Portal, and FEMA Flood Map Service Center.
